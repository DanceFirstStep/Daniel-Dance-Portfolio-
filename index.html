About Me

With over 15 years of professional dance experience, I specialize in Latin dance styles including Salsa, Bachata, and Cha-Cha. My journey began in the vibrant dance communities of Colombia, where I developed a deep passion for the art of movement.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating a supportive, energetic environment where students can discover the joy of dance while developing strong technical skills. I believe dance is a universal language that connects people across cultures.

As a performer and choreographer, I've had the privilege to work with international dance companies and perform at prestigious events worldwide.
